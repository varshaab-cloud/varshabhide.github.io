About me

Bio

I'm Varsha Bhide, a product designer focused on building scalable design systems and thoughtful digital experiences. I care deeply about the craft — the small decisions that make a product feel considered, consistent, and human.

My work lives at the intersection of design thinking and engineering. I enjoy working closely with developers, turning ambiguous problems into clear, buildable solutions — from first principles to production-ready components.

When I'm not designing, I'm thinking about how systems work, what makes a good design token, and why some interfaces feel effortless while others don't.
