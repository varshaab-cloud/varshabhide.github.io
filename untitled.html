Electronic Patient Service

Introducing Electronic Patient Insurance Checker. Streamline your treatment process with real-time insurance verification. Transitioning from traditional physical records to Electronic Health Records (EHR) as a strategic decision aimed at enhancing efficiency, accuracy, sustainability and overall patient care.

Background

Major health insurance providers are designated to offer coverage for specific drugs to recipients. However, a significant hurdle lies in the intricate eligibility determination process, leading to a slow enrollment rate.

As a prominent consulting firm in the healthcare sector aimed to develop a web application for Healthcare Providers (HCPs) to facilitate eligibility checks.

The objective is to enable HCPs and care providers to effortlessly access this information, ensuring they receive the care they deserve and bridging existing gaps.

Problem statement

Create a web application that is interactive, streamlining the eligibility determination process for a specific drug and guaranteeing high acceptance rates

UX strategy

The design process focused on enhancing the usability, accessibility and simplification of the application filling process for Healthcare Providers (HCPs) to achieve the desired results. The designs aimed to streamline the process, making it intuitive, engaging, and ensuring a seamless experience for users.

Brainstorm Solutions

Key Findings

  1. Elaborate and comprehensive application document
  2. Increased rejection rates due to inconsistent or lack of information
  3. The absence of coverage predictions and the cumbersome application process pose challenges in progressing to the next step
  4. Uncertainty around workflow

Design Decisions

  1. Elaborate application simplified by organising it in digestible bitesize sections.
  2. The application will prompt the users if any question is not answered or the answer is not relevant.
  3. Identify if all the steps to get the “benefit report” as the end result and to be personalised for each individual depending on their application status.
  4. The application will help the user through out the process guiding them to achieve the desired result.

Focus areas: HCP’s

  • Gathering accurate information from applicants and caregivers
  • Eliminating redundant data entry across various sections in the application
  • Decreasing the time needed to complete application document
  • Streamlining workload management for increased efficiency, option to delegate the application process at any point of time

Focus areas: Applicant

  • Encourage friendly and approachable interactions
  • Streamline the application process
  • Engage the applicant actively in the process

Wire frames

Coverage Prediction

Coverage prediction involves assessing and predicting the financial protection and benefits that an insurance policy will provide in specific scenarios.

Enrollment

Enrollment is the process of officially registering or signing up for insurance benefits once the predicted coverage has been assessed. This step ensures that individuals, after having an estimate of their coverage, formally enroll to initiate the verification of benefits outlined in their insurance plan.

Prior Authorization

Prior authorization is a per-approval process required by insurance plans to ensure that specific medical services are medically necessary and covered before the services are rendered. It requires to answer a detailed questionnaire and relevant document collection.

Benefit Verification

Benefit verification is a crucial step in the insurance process as it helps both healthcare providers and patients understand the financial aspects of medical services, ensuring transparency and enabling better financial planning
